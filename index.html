Our Story

Cafe 65 Kulhad is inspired by the rich tradition of serving tea in Kulhads - a sustainable and earthy way to enjoy your favorite beverage. We aim to bring you an authentic experience, combined with the warmth of Indian hospitality.

We believe in using fresh, locally sourced ingredients to create a menu that complements our signature Kulhad tea.
